Michael Jackson

~A tribute to the King of Pop~

Singer, dancer, philanthropist - Michael Jackson is regarded as one of the greatest entertainers and cultural icons of the 20th century.

A timeline of Michael Jackson's life:

  • August 29, 1958: Michael is born as the 8th child of Joe and Katherine Jackson.

  • 1964: Michael joins his four older brothers to form the Jackson 5, a pop music group, with famous songs such as "I'll Be There", "I Want You Back" and "ABC".

  • 1971: The prestigious solo career of Michael Jackson begins at the tender age of 13.

  • January 24, 1972: The release date of Michael's first album, "Got To Be There" with Motown Records. He would later break away from the record company to assume greater creative control of his music.

  • June 23, 1972: The film "Ben" debuts, with the theme song of the same name written and performed by Michael which would go on to become the first of 13 MJ hits to reach #1 in the US.

  • August 10, 1979: Michael publishes his first album with Epic Records, "Off The Wall". Michael's songwriting collaboration with the likes of Stevie Wonder and Paul McCartney resulted in the album going platinum.

  • February 27, 1980: For the hit song, "Don't Stop 'Til You Get Enough", Michael receives his first Grammy award for the best male R&B performance of 1979.

  • November 30, 1982: "Thriller" is released and becomes the best-selling album of all time (currently standing at 66 million sales, according to Guiness World Records). The record-breaking album won Michael eight more Grammy awards, including the "Album of The Year" award.

  • December 2, 1983: Michael releases the iconic "Thriller" music video directed by John Landis. This unforgettable work has had such a widespread influence on pop culture, it is the sole music video in the Library of Congress's records.


  • 1987: The 80's hail Michael Jackson as the King of Pop, with additional albums, such as Bad, Dangerous, and HIStory reaching #1 in several countries.

  • February 13, 1997: Michael Joseph Jackson Jr is born, the first of his three children; Paris-Michael Katherine arrived the following year, April 3, 1998 and Prince "Blanket" Michael Jackson II was born February 1, 2002.

  • 2009: The comeback tour "This Is It" is announced. Originally only 10 shows were scheduled for the tour, but an epic demand for tickets called for numerous additional shows, all of which were quickly sold out. Sadly however, Michael would tragically pass away merely weeks before the first show.

  • June 25, 2009: Michael Jackson suffers cardiac arrest induced by acute propofol and benzodiazepine intoxication and dies. VH1 called it the "most shocking music moment of all time". Millions of fans around the world mourned his passing and sales of his music went through the roof.

"Although Michael's life was heartbreakingly short, the fruits of his genius still thrive and the world is a better place for it."


-- Tribupedia - Tribute to the Life of Michael Jackson
